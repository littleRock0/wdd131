The idea originated in 1898 when Utah state representative Billy Gibson commissioned local artist Leo Voight to paint the tribute, paying him $50 for the work. Directions

"From Vernal, get to 500 North and head west to 3500 west. Head north and stay on the road until you arrive at Remember the Maine park. The Flag Crag is the wall across the road from the main park area. Parking is available on the cliff-side, and can accommodate a few cars" - Mountain Project
